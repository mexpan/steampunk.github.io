Hello, everyone. Welcome to the world of steampunk. This is a site that covers the whole story of steampunk. If you're interested in steampunk, it would be a very interesting webpage. So, are you ready to fall in love with the genre of steampunk?

Steampunk is a literary genre derived from Cyberpunk, a subgenre of science fiction in the mid-1980s, and refers to an alternative or similar historical genre that attempted to reinterpret the past by introducing fantastic elements to science and technology in the Victorian and early 20th centuries when modern society was born and Edwardian Britain.

Background music in general

Unlike other SFs, elements other than the 19th century, which are the main background, such as rock music and electric swing, are also used, but the basic themes include classical music that gives a majestic atmosphere and classical feeling, such as music from the Victorian or Belle Époque era, and music from the early 20th century accordion, ragtime, jazz, and swing music that moderately lighten the atmosphere. It is also characterized by the inclusion of elements that make one feel the times, such as the ticking of a mechanical clock, the sound of bells that give the feeling of Big Ben, the sound of steam coming out of a conduit, the sound of winding a spring leaf, and the sound of metal hitting.
